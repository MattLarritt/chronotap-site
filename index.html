ChronoTap

One-tap time tracking

ChronoTap is the fastest way to record where your time goes. Start and stop an activity with a single tap — from the app, your iPhone's Action Button, Siri, Shortcuts, your Apple Watch, or right from the Dynamic Island. No forms, no friction.

Highlights

  • One tap to start, one tap to stop.
  • Track from the Home Screen, Action Button, Siri & Shortcuts, Apple Watch, and the Dynamic Island.
  • Review, adjust times, or add a note later — or auto-confirm routine activities.
  • Daily totals by activity and full history.
  • CSV export for invoicing and reporting.
  • Works offline, syncs privately with iCloud.
